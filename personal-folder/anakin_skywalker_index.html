Anakin Skywalker's status as the Chosen One in the Star Wars universe is a key prophecy that plays a significant role in his character's arc. The prophecy, introduced in the prequel trilogy of Star Wars, foretells the arrival of a being with the potential to bring balance to the Force. The prophecy is described as follows: "A Chosen One will come, born of no father, and through him will ultimate balance in the Force be restored."
